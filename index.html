About Me

I am a dedicated manual tester with 5 years of experience in ensuring software quality and reliability. My expertise lies in identifying bugs, creating test cases, and collaborating with development teams to deliver high-quality products.

I have a strong passion for understanding user needs and ensuring that software meets the highest standards of usability and functionality. My approach to testing is meticulous and user-focused, ensuring that every feature works seamlessly and delivers value to the end user.

Over the years, I have worked on diverse projects across industries such as e-commerce, healthcare, and finance. These experiences have equipped me with the ability to adapt to different domains and understand complex systems quickly.

I am proficient in using tools like JIRA, Trello, and SQL for defect tracking, test management, and database validation. Additionally, I have a foundational understanding of automation tools like Selenium, which helps me collaborate effectively with automation teams.

Outside of work, I enjoy staying updated with the latest trends in software testing and technology. I am also an advocate for continuous learning and often participate in webinars, workshops, and online courses to enhance my skills.
